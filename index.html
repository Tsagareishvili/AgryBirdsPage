First released on December 11, 2009, Angry Birds is a puzzle video game developed by Finnish computer game developer Rovio Entertainment that began the Angry Birds franchise. The gameplay involves slinging birds into pigs' structures in order to defeat them and retrieve their stolen eggs. Inspired primarily by a sketch of stylized wingless birds, the game was first released for iOS and Maemo.
